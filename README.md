# 🚀 Enterprise AI Investment & ROI Dashboard

> An enterprise-grade Business Intelligence solution built using **Power BI**, **Power Query**, and **DAX** to analyze AI investments, monitor operational costs, evaluate ROI, track usage analytics, and identify enterprise risk factors through interactive dashboards.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The rapid adoption of Artificial Intelligence has significantly increased enterprise investments in cloud infrastructure, GPU resources, AI model training, and operational expenses. Organizations require data-driven insights to evaluate investment efficiency, optimize costs, monitor AI adoption, and maximize return on investment (ROI).

This dashboard transforms raw business data into actionable insights through interactive visualizations, KPI monitoring, and advanced analytics.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Monitor enterprise AI investments
- Analyze cloud, GPU, and training costs
- Track AI product usage and adoption
- Measure Return on Investment (ROI)
- Identify high-risk departments and investments
- Support strategic decision-making using interactive dashboards
